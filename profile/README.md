# Black Alsatian

Official GitHub profile for Black Alsatian, a South African web design, development, and digital marketing studio. Trusted since 2003.

## About

Black Alsatian has been building human-centered web experiences since 2003. We help SMEs, startups, and growing brands turn ideas into custom online solutions, high-performance websites, and marketing that convert. (And yes, we also rescue broken WordPress sites when needed.)

## Mission

We craft digital experiences that feel natural, load fast, and guide people to take action. Our work blends design, technology, and marketing so your brand stands out and your website works like a real business asset. We specialize in bespoke software, modern web apps, and robust integrations.
